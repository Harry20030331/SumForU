LumaArc Smart Desk Lamp

Product details

Adjustable LED desk lamp with warm-to-cool color temperature, app scheduling, touch controls, and a built-in wireless phone charger. The lamp has a slim metal arm and a weighted base.

The listing says app setup is optional for basic lighting controls. Advanced schedules and scenes require a 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi connection.

Customer reviews

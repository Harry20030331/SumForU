Northstar Compact Blender

Product details

A 700-watt personal blender with two dishwasher-safe cups, pulse mode, and a footprint designed for small apartment counters. The listing says it handles smoothies, sauces, and crushed ice in small batches.

The motor base is lighter than full-size blenders. The page recommends pausing between heavy blends to avoid overheating.

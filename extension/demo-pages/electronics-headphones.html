Aurora ANC Travel Headphones

Product details

Foldable wireless headphones with active noise cancellation, 34-hour battery life, USB-C fast charging, multipoint Bluetooth, and a compact travel case. The ear cups are memory foam and the app includes three EQ presets.

The headphones weigh 285 grams. The product page highlights strong airplane and commute noise reduction, but notes that the microphone is tuned mainly for indoor calls.

Customer reviews

Review: frequent travelers say the battery lasts through long flights and the case fits easily in a backpack.

Verified customer review: noise cancellation is impressive for train rides, though the headphones feel warm after two hours.

Customer review: call quality is clear in a quiet room but picks up street noise outside.
